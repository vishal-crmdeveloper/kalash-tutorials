❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our programs, schedule, and enrollment process.

What age group and grades do you cater to? ▼

We offer tuition for primary school students from Grade 1 through Grade 6 (ages 6-12). Our curriculum is tailored to each grade level, ensuring age-appropriate teaching methods and content.

How large are your class sizes? ▼

We maintain a strict maximum of 8 students per class. This small group size ensures that every child receives personalized attention and can actively participate in lessons.

What subjects do you teach? ▼

We specialize in three core subjects: Mathematics, Science, and English. Each subject has a structured curriculum with regular assessments to track progress. Students can enroll in one, two, or all three subjects.

What is the class schedule? ▼

We offer flexible scheduling with both weekday and weekend options. Weekday classes run from 9-11 AM or 2-4 PM, and weekend classes run from 9-11 AM or 2-4 PM. You can choose the slot that best fits your child's routine.

How do you track and communicate student progress? ▼

We conduct regular assessments and provide detailed progress reports to parents. Additionally, we hold periodic parent-teacher meetings and maintain an online student portal where parents can track attendance, assignments, and grades.

How do I enroll my child? ▼

You can enroll through our online enrollment form on this website, or visit our center in person. The process takes just a few minutes — you'll need your child's basic details and your contact information. Feel free to call us if you need any help!

Do you offer a trial class? ▼

Yes! We offer a complimentary trial class so your child can experience our teaching style and environment before committing. Contact us to schedule a free trial session in any subject.
